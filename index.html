Greetings Stranger!

Welcome, I am Aidan Clemente originally from State College, PA (Go Nittany Lions!). I spent the last 20 years of my life working in the customer service industry; from bussing tables, to waitressing; high school math tutor, to high school math teacher, I worked and loved to work with the public. Though I enjoyed teaching, I longed for something more. My drive to learn and aptitude for problem solving naturally brought me to programming.

I have a passion for working on the front end. I really enjoy writing JavaScript and working with React. Handlebars was cool, but React is super powerful and I enjoy spending my time learning more about all of the React Magic! In the back end I have experience with Node.js, Express.js, MySQL, writing custom API's, unit testing and functional testing utilizing Mocha and Chai.

I aspire to write clean, concise, and reusable code. I am constantly researching what others are doing and why. I want to understand and learn so I can "do better." I firmly believe that working with and learning from others is how to constantly improve and attain a higher level of understanding. And, only with failures and difficult decisions are we challenged enough to break out of a comfortable place and excel.

I moved to Orlando last year to join the UCF Full Stack Coding Bootcamp 2017/2018 cohort and found my passion in life.
